The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum located in Agra, India. It was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

The construction of the Taj Mahal began in 1632 and was completed in 1648. It is considered one of the most beautiful architectural achievements in the world.

It is also a UNESCO World Heritage Site and attracts millions of tourists every year.
